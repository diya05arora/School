Schools

MISSION

DUV International School is committed to provide a stable, caring and supportive family like environment, where students can receive mature guidance through daily interaction with able faculty and become accountable, productive, and ethical citizens with the skill to think innovatively, reason critically, communicate effectively, and respect peoples of other cultures. DUV International School is also eyeing upon providing such a resourceful learning environment where students can develop into “Citizen of the World” and to develop the learning through differentiated, quality based with a commitment to development and quality teaching.To empower teachers to guide children with means to construct knowledge. To bring back an element of joy to school surrounding which would nudge the child to grow into full bloom. and children be responsible citizen to face challenges of future.

VISION

Our vision at DUV International School is to encourage our students to think, take decisions independently and empowered to acquire value knowledge and skills to become capable, responsible, and dedicated towards a tradition of excellence in this world. full of knowledge, confidence cum passion and cheer and to develop tolerance to lead a happy, meaningful, balanced & graceful life contributing towards progress, prosperity and peace in the world
