Schools

MISSION

DUV International School is committed to provide a stable, caring and supportive family like environment, where students can receive mature guidance through daily interaction with able faculty and become accountable, productive, and ethical citizens with the skill to think innovatively, reason critically, communicate effectively, and respect peoples of other cultures. DUV International School is also eyeing upon providing such a resourceful learning environment where students can develop into “Citizen of the World” and to develop the learning through differentiated, quality based with a commitment to development and quality teaching.To empower teachers to guide children with means to construct knowledge. To bring back an element of joy to school surrounding which would nudge the child to grow into full bloom.

VISION

Our vision at DUV International School is to encourage our students to think, take decisions independently, and be empowered to acquire value, knowledge, and skills to become capable, responsible, and dedicated towards a tradition of excellence in this world. Full of knowledge, confidence, passion, and cheer, we aim to develop tolerance in our students to lead a happy, meaningful, balanced, and graceful life, contributing towards progress, prosperity, and peace in the world. We strive to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures individuality, fosters collaboration, and inspires students to explore their potential while embracing diversity. Through holistic education, we aspire to shape compassionate leaders who will make positive contributions to society and inspire change for the betterment of humanity.
